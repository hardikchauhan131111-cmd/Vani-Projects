❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is वाणी? ▼

वाणी is a student-focused service platform that helps students from Class 6 to College complete their projects, assignments, and creative work. We provide coding, science projects, research reports, design work, and tech solutions with guaranteed 5-day delivery.

What services do you offer? ▼

We offer 5 main categories: Coding & Development (Python, Java, websites, apps), Science Projects (models, experiments), Research & Reports (documentation, PPTs), Creative Design (posters, logos), and Tech Accessories (small apps, tools).

How much does it cost? ▼

Base price starts at ₹150 for Fair quality. Medium quality is ₹175 (+₹25), and Best quality is ₹200 (+₹50). Urgent delivery (under 5 days) adds ₹25. Physical materials for models may have additional costs.

How long does delivery take? ▼

Standard delivery is 5 days via email. For urgent delivery (under 5 days), add ₹25. If we're late, we refund ₹25 automatically.

How do I place an order? ▼

After logging in, click "Place Order", select your service category, fill in project details, choose quality level, apply any discount codes, and complete payment via UPI. You'll receive an Order ID immediately.

Can I track my order? ▼

Yes! Use the "Track Order" page and enter your Order ID. You'll see real-time status: Pending, In Progress, or Order Done. Once delivered, you can also leave a review.

What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept UPI payments only. Scan the QR code on the order page to complete payment.

Can I request changes after delivery? ▼

Yes! You can request edits within 5 days of delivery. Use the "Request Edit" button on the Track Order page, which will send an email to our team with your requirements.

Do you offer refunds? ▼

No refunds are provided. However, if we deliver late, we automatically refund ₹25. Edits are free within 5 days based on your quality level (Fair: 1 edit, Medium: 2 edits, Best: unlimited edits).

Is the work original? ▼

Yes, all work is created specifically for your order. We do not reuse or resell projects. All work is for academic guidance and reference purposes only.

How do discount codes work? ▼

Discount codes are category-specific. After selecting your service, check the discount box for available codes. Enter the code and click "Apply" to get instant savings.

Who are the founders? ▼

Hardik Chauhan (Founder & CEO) and Aayushman Umrao (Co-Founder & CFO) manage all operations, quality control, and customer support.
